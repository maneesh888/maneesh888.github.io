Privacy-First AI Keyboard

Open Keyboard

An iOS keyboard experiment for AI-assisted writing: grammar fixes, rewrites, tone changes, summaries, and practical writing help from inside the keyboard.

Open Keyboard is the client experience: a custom iOS keyboard where you can type normally and ask for AI help when you need grammar fixes, rewrites, tone changes, or summaries.

The privacy direction is that normal typing stays on the device. Text is sent out only when the user chooses an AI action, and it goes to the gateway they configure.

Current status: active prototype. The current focus is product fit, reliable keyboard behavior, privacy copy, and a cleaner path between the app and gateway.

Technical Highlights

Keyboard

Custom iOS Keyboard

The keyboard handles the writing experience: normal typing first, then AI actions when the user asks for help.

Writing

AI Writing Actions

Fix Grammar, Rewrite, Summarize, and related writing tools are routed through the configured backend instead of being hidden inside the keyboard app.

Privacy

User-Controlled Backend

The gateway decides where AI requests go: a local model, a private server, or a hosted AI provider selected by the user.

Testing

Privacy Copy And Product Fit

Privacy And Control

Action

Gateway
